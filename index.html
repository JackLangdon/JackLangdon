Introduction

Hi, my name is John. I'm a developer based out of Melbourne, Australia.

I have a background in Economics and Engineering and currently work as a Web Developer. I really enjoy the process of understanding the personality and culture of a business and finding a way to reflect that through their website.

The world of IT is vast and I enjoy learning new languages and exploring different fields. These days I find myself diving into the world of data and machine learning.

After spending a lot of time working closely with clients to realise their vision, as well as spending time on personal projects, I have come to realise that with IT anything is possible. Where there is an idea, there is a way.

Take a look at some of my work or send me a message in the contact form below if you have any questions, comments or cafe recommendations!
